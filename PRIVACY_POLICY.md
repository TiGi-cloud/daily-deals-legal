## Introduction

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information from Shopify

When you install Daily Deals, we receive and store certain information from your Shopify store through the Shopify API:

**Store Information:**
- Store domain and name
- Store owner contact information
- Plan details

**Product Information:**
- Product titles, descriptions, and IDs
- Product types and tags
- Collection information
- Product pricing (for discount calculations)

**Discount Information:**
- Discount configurations you create
- Scheduled discount settings
- Discount performance data

### 2. Information You Provide

**Configuration Data:**
- Discount schedules you set up
- Product selections
- Collection preferences
- Scheduling intervals

### 3. Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data:**
- App interaction logs
- Error reports
- Performance metrics
- Session information

**Technical Information:**
- IP address
- Browser type
- Device information
- Access times

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
Your data is stored securely on your local infrastructure in Armenia using industry-standard SQLite/PostgreSQL databases.

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

- **Encryption:** Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- **Access Control:** Restricted access to personal data
- **Authentication:** Secure authentication using Shopify OAuth
- **Monitoring:** Regular security audits and monitoring
- **Backups:** Regular automated backups

### Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as:
- You have the app installed, OR
- As required by law or business purposes

After app uninstallation, your data is automatically deleted within 30 days.

## Your Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

1. **Access:** Request copies of your personal data
2. **Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data
3. **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
4. **Restriction:** Request limited processing of your data
5. **Portability:** Request transfer of your data
6. **Objection:** Object to processing of your data

1. Know what personal information is collected
2. Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
3. Opt-out of sale of personal information
4. Request deletion of personal information

## Shopify-Specific Information

### Shopify's Privacy Policy
This app also operates under Shopify's Privacy Policy. Review it at: https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy

### App Scopes
Daily Deals requests the following Shopify permissions:
- `read_products` - To access your product catalog
- `read_product_listings` - To access product collections
- `write_products` - To update product tags for discounts
- `write_discounts` - To create and manage discount codes

### Data Access
We only access data necessary for app functionality. We do not access:
- Customer personal information
- Payment information
- Order details
- Shipping addresses
